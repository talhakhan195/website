ABOUT GLOWDENTAL

Quality Dental Care

At GlowDental, we are committed to providing exceptional dental care in a comfortable and welcoming environment. Our team of highly skilled professionals is dedicated to ensuring the health and well-being of your smile. With years of experience and a passion for the latest advancements in dental technology, we offer a full range of services—from routine cleanings and preventive care to cosmetic dentistry and complex procedures. We believe that every patient deserves personalized treatment and strives to create a stress-free experience for children and adults alike.
At GlowDental, your smile is our top priority, and we look forward to helping you achieve and maintain a healthy, confident smile for years to come.

Meet Our Team

Gary S. Nadler, DDS

Dr. Nadler is a highly skilled and compassionate dentist who graduated from the prestigious New York University College of Dentistry, one of the top dental schools in the nation. With a broad range of expertise, he practices all aspects of dentistry, providing comprehensive care for both adults and children. Whether it's routine checkups, restorative treatments, or more advanced procedures, Dr. Nadler ensures that every patient receives the highest quality care tailored to their specific needs.

Committed to staying at the forefront of the field, Dr. Nadler actively participates in continuing education courses, regularly updating his skills and knowledge with the latest advancements in dental techniques and technology. This dedication allows him to offer state-of-the-art treatments to his patients, ensuring they benefit from the most effective and innovative solutions available.

In particular, Dr. Nadler has a passion for cosmetic dentistry, specializing in smile makeovers that can dramatically improve the appearance of his patients' smiles. He finds immense satisfaction in helping patients transform their smiles, witnessing firsthand the positive impact these changes have on their confidence and self-esteem. The excitement and joy his patients experience when they see their new smiles for the first time is one of the most rewarding aspects of his practice.

Thomas Behm, DDS

Dr. Thomas Behm is a graduate of Muhlenberg College and New York University Dental School. Dr. Behm completed his post graduate residency at the Brookdale Hospital Medical Center in New York. In addition, Dr. Behm actively attends advanced continuing education courses to stay up to date with the most recent advances in dentistry.

In addition to providing basic dental services, Dr. Behm focuses on cosmetic dentistry, especially smile enhancements or complete smile makeovers utilizing porcelain veneers. With an artistic approach and a scientifically proven technique, Dr. Behm is able to make porcelain veneers look completely natural and undetectable.

Dr. Behm provides highly specialized state-of-the-art dental care. He offers a comprehensive array of services including general, cosmetic, and restorative dental procedures. His passion is to change people's lives through dentistry, as he has through the numerous cosmetic makeovers he performs. Dr. Behm believes that a healthy mouth is the gateway to a healthy body.

Dr. Behm is a believer that all patients are entitled to receive quality dental care. With a team of like minded professionals, Dr. Behm started NYC Dental. The goal of NYC Dental is to provide quality and affordable basic dental care in a clean and modern setting to all patients. NYC Dental has grown into 11 multi-specialty dental offices with locations throughout New York including Westchester.

Eric. G Bernstein, DDS

Dr. Bernstein is a highly experienced and dedicated dentist who graduated from the renowned New York University College of Dentistry, a prestigious institution known for its rigorous academic and clinical training. After completing his dental degree, Dr. Bernstein pursued advanced training and specialized education through a postgraduate residency at the Coler-Goldwater Memorial Hospital, one of the leading healthcare facilities. During his time there, he excelled in his field, ultimately serving as the chief resident, where he not only honed his clinical expertise but also demonstrated leadership and mentorship in guiding fellow residents and managing complex patient cases.

Dr. Bernstein is deeply committed to staying up-to-date with the latest dental advancements. He continually participates in continuing education courses, ensuring that his skills are always aligned with the most cutting-edge dental practices and technologies. This commitment to lifelong learning allows him to provide the highest standard of care for his patients, utilizing the most effective and innovative techniques in the field of dentistry.

In addition to his clinical work, Dr. Bernstein is passionate about serving underprivileged communities. He takes great pride in delivering high-quality dental care to both adults and children who may not otherwise have access to the necessary treatment. Whether it's providing preventive care, restorative procedures, or emergency treatments, Dr. Bernstein is dedicated to improving the oral health and well-being of those in need, making a meaningful difference in their lives and ensuring they receive the care they deserve.

Richard Rosen, DDS

Dr. Rosen is a highly accomplished and distinguished dentist with a strong educational foundation and a reputation for excellence in his field. He began his academic journey at Tulane University in New Orleans, Louisiana, where he earned his undergraduate degree. Following his undergraduate studies, Dr. Rosen continued his education at the prestigious New York University College of Dentistry, one of the top dental schools in the country. At NYU, he not only excelled academically but also demonstrated exceptional clinical skills, graduating at the top of his class. His outstanding achievements were recognized through his induction into the esteemed Omicron Kappa Upsilon dental honor society, a distinction reserved for the top graduates in the field of dentistry.

During his time at NYU, Dr. Rosen was awarded the prestigious Award for Excellence in Restorative Dentistry, further showcasing his expertise in this important aspect of dental care. After completing his dental education, Dr. Rosen pursued additional training in general dentistry through a residency at the renowned New York Methodist Hospital in Brooklyn. This residency provided him with invaluable hands-on experience in a wide range of dental disciplines, allowing him to refine his skills in providing comprehensive care to patients with diverse needs.
